7 * Create a functional game of BattleShip, implementing many of the programming
8 * concepts from Chapter 13 to 16, while following the rules of the original
9 * board game which are:
10 *
11 * 1. Players place their 5 ships on their player board, the rules here are:
12 *
13 * - Place each ship in any horizontal or vertical position, but not
14 * diagonally.
15 * - Do not place a ship so that it overlaps the edge of the board or another
16 * ship.
17 * - Do not change the position of any ship once the game has begun.
18 *
19 * 2. Decide who will go first, each player will alternate turns, calling out
20 * one shot per turn to try and hit each other's ships.
21 *
22 * 3. On your turn, pick a target hole on your enemy board and call out its
23 * location by the letter and number. Each target hole has a letter-number
24 * coordinate that corresponds with the same coordinate on your
25 * enemy's board. To determine each coordinate, find its
26 * corresponding letter on the left side of the target board and its number
27 * on the top of the board. For example, when you call a shot, your opponent
28 * must tell you whether your shot is a hit or a miss.
29 *
30 * 4. It's a Hit - If you call out a shot location that is occupied by a ship on
31 * your enemy's board, your shot is a hit! Your opponent tells you which ship
32 * you have hit (cruiser, submarine, etc.). Your shots will be recorded for
33 * you on each of the boards. Your opponent's board will be updated in the
34 * corresponding hole of the ship you have hit on his or her board.
35 *
36 * 5. It's a Miss - If you call out a shot location not occupied by a ship on
37 * your opponent's board, it's a miss. The board will record this move, so
38 * you cannot call this shot again.
39 *
40 * Play continues in this manner, with you and your opponent calling one shot
41 * per turn.
42 *
43 * 6. Once all the holes in any one ship are hit then the ship is considered
44 * sunk. The owner of the ship must announce which ship was sunk.
45 *
46 * 7. If you're the first player to sink your opponents entire fleet of 5 ships,
47 * you win the game!
48 *
49 */
